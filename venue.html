Conference location

The conference will again be held in Stevenson Hall at Illinois State University in Normal, Illinois.

Arriving by air

Direct to Bloomington-Normal

You'll travel to Bloomington's Central Illinois Regional Airport (CIRA). Airport code: BMI.

Via Chicago O'Hare (ORD) or Midway (MDW)

You can often find cheaper tickets (especially for international travel) traveling through Chicago. From there, you can take a shuttle bus (the Peoria Charter) directly from the airport to the Amtrak Station in Normal. The shuttle service takes around three hours each way. The Amtrak station is four blocks east of Stevenson Hall.
